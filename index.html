Information about Solar Panels:

The energy of the sun is now able to be captured by engineers and scientists. The source is the Sun- what we just talked about. Fusion of nuclear fuel produces solar energy. The sun undergoes nuclear fusion which is the process of the Sun burning the hydrogen atoms, which make the helium atoms. The Sun fuses about 600 million tons of hydrogen to make helium per day! The protons of hydrogen atoms fiercely collide during this nuclear fusion, fusing to form helium atoms at the sun's core. This procedure is referred to as a proton-proton chain reaction, and it produces a significant quantity of energy.
